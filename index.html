ABOUT US

   "ANAKHRONOS", THE ANNUAL CULTURAL FESTIVAL OF TECHNO INDIA, IS HELD IN FEBRUARY-MARCH EVERY YEAR. IT IS ONE OF THE BIGGEST FEST IN KOLKATA WITH A CUMULATIVE FOOTFALL OF 20,000 AND ABOVE IN A SPAN OF 2 DAYS.

   ANAKHRONOS WAS STARTED BY A GROUP OF YOUNG ENTHUSIASTS IN THE YEAR 2003, AND IT HAS NOW BECOME A TRADITION. IN PAST, ANAKHRONOS HAS BEEN A STAGE FOR THE MOST UNIQUE AND ENTERTAINING PERFORMANCES IN INDIA.

   SOME OF THE MOST OUTSTANDING PERFORMANCES OVER THE PAST FEW YEARS ARE THOSE BY "EUPHORIA, STRINGS, PENTAGRAM, KK, SHAAN, KUNAL GANJAWALA, FOSSILS, PARIKRAMA, SPUNK, AXE-TORTION, USHA UTHUP, CHANDRABINDOO, ABHIJEET, PARASHPATHOR, CACTUS, ASH KING, MD IRFAN, RAETH AND MANY MANY MORE".

   ANAKHRONOS IS DERIVED FROM THE GREEK WORD ANACHRONISMS WHICH CAN BE INTERPRETED AS "MOMENTS SURPASSING TIME"... AND YES, WE DO LIVE UP TO ITS MEANING..! IT IS INDEED ONE OF THE MOST PROMISING OPPORTUNITIES FOR STUDENTS TO STAGE THEIR TALENTS. THIS YEAR BEING THE 13TH VERSION, WE PROMISE TO MAKE IT BIGGER... BETTER... LARGER THAN EVER BEFORE!

BAND-BOX

Band Competition

DATE: 6TH MARCH 2016

TIME: 11 AM

BANDBOX

DO YOU BREATHE MUSIC, LIVE MUSIC AND DIE FOR MUSIC? DO YOU BELIEVE YOU CAN CREATE MUSIC LIKE NO ONE ELSE CAN? THIS IS YOUR CHANCE TO BE THE BAND OF YOUR DREAMS. LET LOOSE YOUR IMAGINATION AND SPREAD YOUR WINGS. MAKE SURE, YOU LAND RIGHT AT THE STAGE OF ANAKHRONOS. WE ARE WAITING!!!

Registration Fees :
Prizes worth ₹ to be won. Special Goodies also to be given away.

    Rules
  1. Team Size: 4-10 members. At least 1 vocalist and 3 different instruments required (different guitars counted individually) and one percussion instrument compulsory.

  9. Team Anakhronos will not be responsible for failure of any instruments.
  10. Team Anakhronos reserves the right to make any last minute changes in rules and has the right to disqualify a participating band on grounds of misconduct.
